What is this thesis about?


This thesis proposes methods to solve following problems faced during explorations through quadcopters.

Mosaicing Scenes With Vacant Spaces

One of the major contribution of this piece of the thesis is a method to create a mosaic of a scene containing vacant spaces using fusion of a standard stitching algorithm with positional data captured from a quadcotper. Read more ...

Imaging Multiplanar Scenes

We have many circumstances where the input scene is spread over multiple planes. In such cases, we would like to image each planar region orthographically and then 'unroll' the whole scene by joining the individual mosaics so that we get the output mosaic of the input scene as if it is present on a single plane. Read more ...

Detecting Fiducials Under Blur

A single quadcopter is not sufficient for imaging large multiplanar scenes due to energy constraints. Instead, we may use multiple quadcopters in collaboration for imaging multiplanar scenes.
