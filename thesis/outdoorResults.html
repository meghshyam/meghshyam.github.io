Autonomous Imaging of Outdoor Scenes

We have done experiments on large outdoor scenes spread over single planar surface. In one such experiment, there was a painting on a large wall (15 feet height) as shown in Figure 1 (Left).
As the wall was too tall and it was not possible to view the full wall in one flash due to a shortage of space, we selected the desired area in two steps as shown in Figure 1(Middle). In path planning stage, 37 positions (12 for the top part and 25 for the bottom part) were estimated to encompass the user-selected area. Images captured from those positions are mosaiced using our algorithm to get the final mosaicing output as shown in Figure 1(Right).
