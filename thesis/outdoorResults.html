Autonomous Imaging of Outdoor Scenes

In this sample, we demonstrate results on a large outdoor scene. A 15 feet wall has a painting from an urban artist as shown in the long range photograph Figure 1 on the left.
To demonstrate path planning with multiple visits, the quadcopter explored the wall twice as indicated in the middle section. The path planning stage resulted in 37 positions at which images were captured. There were 12 for the first visit (top), and 25 for the second part. Images captured from those positions are individually mosaiced, and then combined to get the final mosaicing output as shown in Figure 1(Right).
